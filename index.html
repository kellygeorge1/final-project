    • Our Story

    Our story begins first at Big Bob's Diner when my Bobby would make burgers with his father. Bob wanted to be creative with his burgers beginning with the first one: Baby, You Can Chive My Car Burger. It had pickles as wheels... Big Bob did not like the creative burgers and little Bob didn't want to work there any longer. Then Bob and I married, opened the restuarant, and had a few kids in the meantime. Bob's Burgers is now family owned and run by Bob and I, Linda. Our three babies, Tina, Gene, and Louise, help in the restaurant. Even Handyman Teddy will be in the restaurant most of the day. DO NOT sit on his stool though or it will be a loooooong day. Come visit us near the Boardwalk!

    P.S. There is no human meat in our bugers!! Even if a certain Health Inspector says there is. He is a liar. I hope he doesn't see this. IF YOU'RE SEEING THIS, HUGO, WE DO NOT USE HUMAN MEAT. There was an incident one time with horse meat, but we fixed that. Only a few people ate it. It's fine. They're alive.
